CIA Triad & Personal Security

CyberOps Academy — 30-Day Analyst Bootcamp Portfolio

Confidentiality

Ensuring that sensitive information is only accessible by those authorized to view it.

My Example: Protecting my banking credentials and UPI PIN from unauthorized disclosure.

Integrity

Guaranteeing that data remains accurate and has not been tampered with or altered.

My Example: Ensuring my academic grades at BCU remain accurate and trustworthy.

Availability

Making sure that systems, networks, and data are always ready for use when needed.

My Example: Keeping the college LMS and exam portals online during submission deadlines.

Top 3 Personal Threats

Phishing Attacks: Deceptive messages aiming to steal credentials via fake login pages.
Device Loss/Theft: Physical loss of hardware leading to unauthorized access to stored data.
Weak Password Hygiene: Reusing simple passwords across multiple vulnerable accounts.

My Security Mindset

  • I will think like a defender before trusting any digital interaction.
  • I will protect every account with MFA and unique, strong passwords.
  • I will verify data integrity before assuming information is correct.
  • I will maintain redundant backups to ensure constant availability.
  • I will treat cybersecurity as a continuous personal habit.
